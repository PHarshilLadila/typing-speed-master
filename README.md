## üéØ How It Works

### Getting Started

1. **Visit the Application**
   - Open the web application in your browser - https://typingspeedmaster.vercel.app
   - No installation required - runs entirely in your browser

2. **Sign In (Optional)**
   - Click "Sign In with Google" to save your progress
   - Guest mode available for quick tests without authentication

3. **Configure Your Test**
   - Select your preferred difficulty level
   - Choose test duration
   - Customize any additional settings

4. **Start Typing**
   - Press Enter or Space to begin your typing test!
   - Type the displayed text as quickly and accurately as possible
   - Watch your metrics update in real-time

5. **View Results**
   - See your WPM, accuracy, and consistency scores
   - Navigate to the dashboard for detailed analytics

### Understanding Your Metrics

**Words Per Minute (WPM)**
- Measures your typing speed
- Calculated based on correct characters typed
- Higher is better

**Accuracy**
- Percentage of correctly typed characters
- Reflects precision and attention to detail
- Target: 95%+ for proficient typing

**Consistency**
- Measures how steady your typing speed is
- Lower variation means better consistency
- Important for long-form typing tasks

**Difficulty Levels**
- **Easy**: Common words, simple sentences
- **Medium**: Standard vocabulary, varied sentence structure
- **Hard**: Complex words, technical terms

## Troubleshooting

**Authentication Issues**
- Ensure pop-ups are enabled for Google sign-in
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

**Performance Issues**
- Check your internet connection
- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Update to the latest browser version

**Data Not Saving**
- Verify you're signed in with Google
- Check browser's local storage permissions
- Ensure stable internet connection
